A police chase in West Montgomery ends with a fatal accident. Officials say a state trooper attempted to pull over a car and the chase began. The suspect crashed his vehicle on Old Selma Road in Montgomery, and two other cars crashed as a result. The suspect's car caught fire and he died in the crash. Two other people were also taken to the hospital with minor injuries.

Several gallons of fuel spilled into Catoma Creek. Fire fighters say they were able to remove all of it.

Officials still aren't sure why the man ran. The man who died is identified as 27 year old Arthur Moss of Montgomery.
