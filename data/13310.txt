SHELBY COUNTY, TX (KTRE) - One person was killed and another is in critical condition after a one-vehicle accident in Shelby County Saturday night.

The accident happened on State Highway 7 near FM 711 around 6:35 PM Saturday.



DPS says Robin Louise Griffin, 37, of Lufkin was driving a 1997 Ford 4-door eastbound on State Highway 7 when the vehicle left the roadway and overturned.

Griffin's passenger, Kristiana Gamboa, 19, of Shelbyville died at the scene. Gamboa was not wearing a seatbelt.



Griffin is in critical condition at LSU hospital in Shreveport with severe fractures and a severed foot. Griffin was also not wearing a seatbelt.
