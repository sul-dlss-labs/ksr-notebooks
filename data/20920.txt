Battle Creek Enquirer

A 22-year-old Battle Creek man is dead after a police chase at 12:49 a.m. Monday in Emmett Township.

Zachary Phillips was riding a motorcycle when he was stopped by a state trooper, acknowledged the officer and then fled, according to a news release from Michigan State Police.

After a brief pursuit, the motorcycle left a curve on East Columbia Avenue and crashed. Phillips was pronounced dead at the scene. The crash is under investigation.

Calhoun County sheriff's deputies and Emmett Township Public Safety Officers assisted at the scene.
