A 17-year-old boy is dead and another teen is seriously injured following a police pursuit on the city's Southwest Side.

About 1:43 a.m. Wednesday, police officers were chasing a Toyota Camry near South Central Park Avenue and West 32nd Street during a "traffic pursuit," said Chicago Police News Affairs Officer Ron Gaines.

Police could not immediately say what prompted the chase, but at some point, the officers stopped pursuing the car, Gaines said. Police said the car was stolen.

The driver, in trying to flee, crashed into a concrete barrier in the 3200 block of West 32nd Street, Gaines said.

A 17-year-old boy was pronounced dead on the scene, and another 17-year-old boy was taken to Mount Sinai Hospital in serious condition.

No officers were injured during the pursuit, and the department's Major Accidents Unit was investigating, Gaines said.
