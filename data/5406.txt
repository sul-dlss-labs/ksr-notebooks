A man died in police custody yesterday after complaining about a heart problem and swollen legs, the police said. The man, Ronald Clemons, 45, of Bushwick, had been arrested and charged with driving with a suspended license and criminal possession of a controlled substance shortly after 7 p.m. He was taken to a hospital, but was returned to Brooklyn Central Booking, where he later collapsed. He was taken to Long Island College Hospital, where he was declared dead on arrival.
