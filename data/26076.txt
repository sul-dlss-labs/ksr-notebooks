GARDENA — A suspect armed with a weapon reportedly charged officers in Gardena on Monday night and was fatally wounded in an officer-involved shooting. No officers were injured.

Firefighters responded to the 1700 block of Rosecrans Avenue, just east of Western Avenue, at 9:55 p.m. and located the wounded suspect, who was taken to a hospital in critical condition, according to the Los Angeles County Fire Department.

The Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department, which sent LASD Homicide Bureau detectives to assist the Gardena Police Department in the investigation, said the shooting happened at the intersection of Rosecrans and Denker avenues and that the suspect was a man.

“The suspect was transported to a local hospital where he was pronounced dead,” an LASD statement said.

Police went to the area because of a call of a person with a weapon and when they arrived, the suspect charged at officers, who opened fire, KCAL9 reported.
