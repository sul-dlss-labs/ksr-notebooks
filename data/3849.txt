From Times Staff and Wire Reports

Police on Thursday identified a man shot to death by officers as Brian Charles Smith, 35, of Placentia.

Police began receiving calls at 5 p.m. about a man “acting strangely” at a convenience store on Orangethorpe Avenue, said Det. Corinne Loomis.

Loomis said officers attempted to subdue Smith with a Taser, but he remained combative and was shot about 5:15 p.m. in the 100 block of El Camino Lane.
