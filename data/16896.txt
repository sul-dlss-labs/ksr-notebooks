The man shot to death by a Riverside County sheriff’s deputy during a struggle at a Jurupa Valley park was identified Monday, Nov. 16, as Ernesto Gamino, 25, of Mira Loma.

The confrontation happened about 5 p.m. on Friday at Rancho Mira Loma Park.

A deputy responding to a report of a disturbance got into a fight with the man and fired. The deputy suffered minor injuries and was treated at a hospital. The man died at a hospital.

Anyone with information about the incident is asked to call the Jurupa Valley sheriff’s station at 951-955-2600.
