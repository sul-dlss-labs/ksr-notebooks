LEE’S SUMMIT, Mo. — One person is dead after an officer-involved shooting in Lee’s Summit on Sunday.

Police were dispatched to the 200 block of NW Hickory Street on a domestic disturbance call at approximately 12:40 p.m.

Authorities said when an officer arrived at the residence, the suspect charged out the front door toward the officer with a large knife in his hand. The officer then fired his gun at the suspect, striking him in the chest.

The suspect was transported to an area hospital, where he died of his injury.

Police identified the man as 34-year-old Jeffrey K. Lanahan.

The officer, a 24-year veteran of the Lee’s Summit Police Department, has been placed on routine administrative leave with the incident is investigated.
