LOUISVILLE, KY (WAVE) - WAVE 3 News has learned the identity of the man killed after being tased by Metro Police Wednesday night.

Around 10:30 p.m., officers responded to the 5000 block of Delaware Drive to break up a fight. Police say while trying to separate 34 year old Isaac Bass and his brother, Bass attacked one of the officers with a flashlight. From there the confrontation with Bass escalated.

"There was a struggle over officer's firearm. These officers still used restraint and used less than lethal force," said Sgt. Robert Biven, a Metro Police spokesperson.

Bass was transported to Norton Suburban Hospital where he died.

The two officers involved, 15 year veteran Keith Heselschwerdt and Larry Wagner, have been placed on administrative duty while an investigation is conducted.
