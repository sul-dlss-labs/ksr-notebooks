From Times Staff Reports

Authorities have released the names of two men killed when the car they were in was hit during a police pursuit involving a stolen car.

Aaron M. Keller, 33, and Jacob S. Bertucci, no age available, were killed Friday night when their 2002 Nissan collided with another car after being struck by a stolen 2001 Volkswagen, the California Highway Patrol said.

The collision occurred shortly after 9:30 p.m. at Odgen Drive and Fountain Avenue.
