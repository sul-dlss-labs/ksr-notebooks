DORA, Ala. (WBRC) - Two Dora Police officers and a suspect were transported to area hospitals after an officer-involved shooting Saturday morning.

ALEA officers confirmed Monday the suspect had died.

The incident occurred in the 500 block of High St.

The officers’ injuries are reported as minor.

The investigation is ongoing. Once complete, findings will be turned over to the Walker County District Attorney’s Office.
