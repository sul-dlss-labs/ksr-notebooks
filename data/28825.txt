A man pulled from the Milwaukee River by divers Saturday night has died, police said Sunday.

The man, 28, jumped in the river while fleeing on foot from Milwaukee County sheriff's deputies around 6:10 p.m. in the 100 block of West Clybourn Street, Milwaukee police said in news release.

Milwaukee Fire Department divers were called at 6:22 p.m., entered the water at 6:36 and pulled the victim from the water 10 minutes later, Acting Deputy Chief DeWayne Smoots said. Witnesses reported seeing the man jump in the river.
