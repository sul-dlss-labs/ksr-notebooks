Kentucky State Police released its final report on a shooting incident involving a Scott County deputy.

, the Scott County Sheriff's Office reported that Deputy Jaime Morales was hit by a bullet from a law enforcement weapon.

A shootout happened as deputies, Georgetown Police and federal agents tried to take Florida fugitive Edward Reynolds into custody.

"Shots fired, shots fired. Shots fired, central shots fired. Get EMS up to us now." This was heard on scanner traffic during the shootout in September.

Investigators said Morales broke a window of a vehicle where Reynolds was sitting. That is when Reynolds allegedly pulled out a gun and police opened fire, killing him.

Morales was hit by friendly fire and is now paralyzed. Investigators do not know which officer fired the shot that injured him.
