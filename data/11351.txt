Police have identified the man killed during a police-involved shooting in Southwest Miami-Dade Thursday.

Sergio Javier Azcuy, 46, of Hialeah, was shot and killed by a detective after he refused to comply during an investigation on SW 120 Street and 137 Avenue, Miami-Dade Police said.

The incident began after authorities were pursing two suspects as part of a robbery investigation, officials said. Police conducted a traffic stop on a green Kia and a confrontation ensued that led the detective to shoot Azcuy, according to a police report.

The second suspect, whose name has not been released, was taken into custody and is being questioned by authorities, according to officials. Charges are still pending.
