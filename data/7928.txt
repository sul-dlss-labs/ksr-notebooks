Reported by: KPLC Staff

LAKE CHARLES, LA (KPLC) - The Lake Charles police officer involved in Monday's fatal crash is identified as Officer David Bray.

The accident happened at the intersection of 12th St. and 3rd Ave.

Authorities say Officer Bray was heading east on 12th St., when his car collided with a car driven by 82-year-old Jim McIntosh.

McIntosh died from the crash.

Lake Charles police say Officer Bray has been with the Lake Charles Police Department since August 2007.
