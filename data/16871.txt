OKLAHOMA CITY — A traffic stop at 36th and Lincoln took a terrible turn Sunday.

Police stopped a man in a black SUV they thought was driving under the influence.

The man reportedly took off and crashed the vehicle into a concrete median on 36th and Kelley. The SUV reportedly rolled several times and came to rest about 50 yards from the intersection.

Police say the passenger of the car was ejected and the driver was pinned.

Both victims died at the scene, according to officials.

The victims were later identified as 49-year-old Lionel Shoate and 21-year-old Sakeyla Harris.

Officials say Shoate was driving the car and Harris was in the passenger’s seat.
