Editor’s note: This story has been updated throughout.

A Progreso police officer fatally shot a machete-wielding man Sunday night, according to a news release from the Hidalgo County Sheriff’s Office.

At about 9:24 p.m., Sheriff’s deputies responded to a home on Encino Drive at the request of the Progreso Police Department.

Upon arrival, deputies say they they found a man dead with a gunshot wound.



Investigators spoke to witnesses, who said a Progreso police officer responded to the area after reports of a man wielding a machete.

According to the news release:

“The officer made contact with the male subject and a struggle ensued. During the struggle, the officer discharged his weapon fatally shooting the male. The officer was transported to a local hospital for treatment of non life threatening injuries.”

An investigation into the escalation of use of force continues.

The man’s identity will be released pending notification of next of kin.

The Hidalgo County Sheriff’s Office asks anyone with information regarding the ongoing investigation to call the Criminal Investigations Division at 956-383-8114.
