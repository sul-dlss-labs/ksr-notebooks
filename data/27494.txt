ALBUQUERQUE, N.M. (KRQE) – The Albuquerque Police Department is investigating what led up to a deadly crash on east Central involving a suspected stolen car on Tuesday. Police say around 6 p.m. officers tried to pull over a silver sedan, near Central and Juan Tabo.

The car took off, jumping the median and crashed into a pickup in the oncoming lane. The driver of the pickup was not hurt. A woman in the sedan died and the man with her took off running. Central was closed on Tuesday night as police investigated.

The driver of the crash remains on the loose. Police have not yet been able to identify the suspect. They also have not released the woman’s name.
