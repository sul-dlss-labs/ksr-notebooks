One person was killed following a chase and a crash in Big Spring on Tuesday night, according to the Big Spring Police Department.

Officials say officers in the area of FM 700 noticed a motorcycle exiting off the south service road in front of the patrol vehicle.

The driver of the motorcycle was reportedly driving recklessly and failed to signal while turning onto S. Goliad.

The officer then tried to conduct a traffic stop but say the driver of the motorcycle evaded officers and crashed in the 2000 block of 3rd Street.

The driver, Austin Ryan Cantu, was found dead at the scene of the crash, according to the release.

A passenger on the motorcycle, Ellen Alexandra Vasquez was found in serious condition and transported to Scenic Mountain Medical Center, police say.

She was later airlifted to University Medical Center in Lubbock.

Police officials say the investigation is ongoing.
