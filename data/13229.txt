CENTRAL HEIGHTS — A Lauderdale County Sheriff’s deputy fatally shot a man who pointed a loaded rifle at him when he and other deputies came to the man’s residence Saturday night to investigate a neighbor’s complaint, authorities said.

The shooting occurred shortly after 9 p.m., Lauderdale County Sheriff Ronnie Willis said.

Robert Bobo said the man who was killed was his grandson, Mitchell Keith Campbell, 31.
