CALHOUN COUNTY, MI -- A 28-year-old Springfield man died after his vehicle crashed into a tree as he fled from police north of Battle Creek.

Police identified the man as Thomas Allen Clearwater.

Calhoun County sheriff's deputies said police tried to stop the man's vehicle on Michigan Avenue near West Vanburen Street about 3 a.m. Saturday, May 6.

The vehicle was in the oncoming traffic lane.

The driver did not stop and instead fled, heading north of Waubascon Road. Deputies pursued the vehicle.

The pursuit started in Springfield and continued about six miles until the crash happened.

The vehicle went off the road in a turn near Waubascon Lake and struck a tree.

Police said they suspect alcohol was a factor in the crash.
