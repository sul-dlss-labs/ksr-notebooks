Staff report

NEWARK TOWNSHIP - Police have closed Price Road in both directions as they investigate a shooting with two fatalities.

Few details had been released about the incident as of 9:10 p.m. Thursday.

Sheriff Randy Thorp confirmed deputies are on scene investigating a "double fatal shooting."

Medics were called to the 4300 block of Price Road shortly before 8:15 p.m. Thursday. No medical transports were made from the scene and detectives were called.

The Ohio Bureau of Criminal Investigation has also been called to the scene, per scanner traffic.

Thorp told a reporter from The Advocate on the scene that deputies are not actively searching for a suspect in relation to the shooting. No additional details are expected to be released Thursday.
