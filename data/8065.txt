Montgomery, AL (WSFA) - The Alabama State Trooper injured in Thursday's deadly police chase on Interstate 65, is home now recovering.

The trooper was involved in a hit and run accident, during a high speed chase.

Troopers opened fire after the suspect hit the officer, causing the suspect to crash into a guardrail. The driver, 59-year-old John Mccullough of Bay Minette died at the scene.
