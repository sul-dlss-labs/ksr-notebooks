ADAMS COUNTY, Colo. — One suspect was killed and another is in the hospital after an officer-involved shooting at the Timber Ridge Apartments on Thursday afternoon, the Adams County Sheriff’s Office said.

Deputies responded to a report of a suspicious vehicle with three people inside near West 69th Avenue and Mariposa Street . The driver of the vehicle is being questioned.

There were two female passengers inside the vehicle and one pulled a gun on a deputy, the sheriff’s office said.

One woman was pronounced dead at the scene and the other is in the hospital.
