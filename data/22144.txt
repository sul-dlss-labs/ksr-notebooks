A Williamstown man died in the custody of Lawnside Police Thursday night, the Camden County Prosecutor's Office said.

Investigators from the Camden County Prosecutor's Office are reviewing the circumstances that led to the death of Rodney Terry, 47, of Gloucester County.

Terry was arrested about 6:30 p.m. Thursday after police observed him throwing rocks and yelling at a home on Charleston Avenue in Lawnside. Terry was handcuffed and placed in the rear of a police cruiser, according to the prosecutor's office.

The officers called EMS because of his behavior. When the EMTs removed him from the vehicle he was not breathing, officials said.

He was pronounced dead at Kennedy Hospital in Stratford at 7:53 p.m.

The prosecutor's office said it will release the cause and manner of Terry's death when it is available.
