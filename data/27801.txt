The Louisiana State Police Bureau of Investigations Monroe Field Office is investigating an officer-involved shooting in Ouachita Parish.

Around 10:40 a.m. Thursday morning, investigators responded to a deputy-involved shooting in Swartz.

LSP MFO was requested to investigate the shooting by OPSO.

A preliminary investigation revealed OPSO responded to a disturbance call regarding a person who threatened another man with a firearm.

Upon arrival, deputies encountered the subject, identified as Nathan R. Hodge, 66, of Monroe. During the course of the investigation, shots were fired resulting in the death of Hodge.

No deputies were injured during this incident.

No additional information is available at this time.
