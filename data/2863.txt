State Police are now investigating the shooting death of a man by an Amite police officer. Amite Police Chief Chuck Gabriel says his officers were trying to arrest a man convicted of manslaughter on a parole violation. The incident happened Thursday evening. Chief Gabriel says when approached by his officers the suspect fled down West Pine Street and headed into a wooded area.

The chief says during the chase one of his officers felt threatened and shot the suspect. The suspect died at the hospital. The police officer, who's name is being withheld, is suspended with pay while state police investigate the matter. The suspect's name is also being withheld until all family members have been notified.
