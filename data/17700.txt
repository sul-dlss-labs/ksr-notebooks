Around 1:40 p.m. Wednesday, police say two Oklahoma City police bike patrol officers made contact with a man near Sheridan and Western.

Officials say the man refused to follow officers verbal directions and then produced a knife.

The man allegedly made threatening gestures towards the officers.

That is when MSgt. Steve Helmer shot the suspect.

That man has been identified as 43-year-old Derek Allan Prophet of California.

Officials believe the man may have been homeless; however, it has not been confirmed at this time.
