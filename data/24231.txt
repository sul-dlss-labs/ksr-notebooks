From Staff Reports

A four-hour police standoff ended in the suspect's suicide early Sunday, police said.

Adam Setzer, 37, was pronounced dead at the scene at 9221 S Shartel Ave. at the Brookwood Village Apartments.

Setzer had told his mother he was going to kill himself and she called 911 after seeing him with a gun, police report.

Officers talked by telephone with Setzer for about four hours, police report.

Setzer became agitated, and during a telephone conversation he fired several rounds out of an apartment window. A tactical team responded to the scene and Setzer then shot himself in the head, police report.
