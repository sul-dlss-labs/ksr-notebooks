(WMC-TV) - The second man who escaped from the Jackson County Jail died in a car crash Tuesday, according to the NBC affiliate in Little Rock.

The Howell County Sheriff's Office in Missouri reported that while leading authorities on a chase, 39-year-old Steven Mitchell crashed his vehicle and died.

Mitchell and Steven Reed escaped from the Jackson County Jail on Monday morning.

Reed was caught Monday evening.

Reed has been charged with felony first-degree escape. He is back in the Jackson County Jail on $1 million bond, according to KARK.
