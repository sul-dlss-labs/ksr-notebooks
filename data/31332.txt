Cristian Alanzo Anguiano, 32, of Corona, was shot by officers at the scene of a reported family disturbance at around 8:15 p.m. Sunday in the 1000 block of West Rancho Road, according to Corona Police Department Cpl. Tobias Kouroubacalis.

Officers arrived on the scene to find Anguiano in the driveway, refusing to comply with commands from law enforcement, police said. According to Kouroubacalis, the man reentered the residence and forced his way into the bedroom where family members were hiding from him.
