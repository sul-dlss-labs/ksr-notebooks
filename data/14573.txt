A Columbus man died as a result of injuries sustained in a motorcycle accident near the intersection of U.S. Highway 84 and Brannon Stand Road Friday evening.

Garrett Parker, 21, of Columbus, Ga., was pronounced dead at Flowers Hospital around 7:15 p.m. Friday, Houston County Coroner Robert Byrd said.

Parker had been involved in a high-speed chase with police prior to the accident, Byrd said. The chase began in Henry County and was later taken up by Midland City police, Byrd said.

The chase ended when Parker lost control of his motorcycle and was thrown from the vehicle near the intersection of U.S. Highway 84 and Brannon Stand Road, Byrd said.
