A man who police say lead authorities on a chase is dead.

It all started in Russellville around 1:00 am Thursday.

According to investigators, Russellville Police Officers spotted a stolen truck and tried to pull over the driver, but the driver wouldn't stop.

Once the chase came to an end, Police say the driver became violent injuring two officers. Those officers were treated for minor injuries.

The driver was taken to a hospital in Russellville. WAFF 48 News has learned the man died about an hour after getting to the hospital.

Police identify the man as John Morgan.
