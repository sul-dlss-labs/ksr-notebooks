Watauga police shot and killed a man who had been ordered to stay away from a home where a woman was later found dead inside, authorities said Monday.

A woman also was found dead inside the home. Police said she appeared to be the victim of a homicide.

The Tarrant County medical examiner's office identified the woman as Emily Law, 49. She was found dead about 11:50 a.m. Monday, but her cause of death wasn't specified in online records as of Tuesday afternoon.

The man was identified as James Freeman, 46, who died of a gunshot wound to the chest. Medical examiner's office records gave a Fort Worth address for Freeman.

Police did not provide information about Law's relationship to Freeman.
