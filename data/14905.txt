The Oakland County Medical Examiner’s office has identified the man who died after a 2000 Jeep he was driving crashed on southbound I-75 following a police chase in Hazel Park on Thursday.

The man has been identified as 17-year-old DeAngelo Woods. The medical examiner’s office said Woods is listed as a Detroit resident.

Earlier, Hazel Park police said the vehicle Woods was driving was stolen from Madison Heights. The police there were two other suspects involved in the chase in a different vehicle. Police have one of them in custody and are searching for the other.
