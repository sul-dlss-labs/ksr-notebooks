One man is dead, two others are injured after a police chase in Abbeville. According to police, officers were chasing a car up Highway 95 north toward Barbour County. The car being chased crashed into a second vehicle. Police say the man driving the speeding car died at the scene. Three others were hurt, but were told their injuries are not life-threatening. At this time police are not releasing the man's name. Officers were working a drug investigation when the chase began.
