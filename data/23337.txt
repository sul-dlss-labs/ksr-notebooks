Jackson police were involved a fatal shooting on Wednesday afternoon, according to the Jackson Police Department's Twitter.

The incident happened some time before 5:30 p.m. on Mayes Street near Lampton Avenue.

The victim was armed with a knife and approached an officer, JPD said. An officer fired a single shot striking the man in the chest. Per protocol, the officer was placed on administrative leave with pay, police said.

No other information has been released.

The investigation is ongoing.
