DANVILLE -- An exchange of gunfire in Danville has left two men dead, one possibly the result of police fire.

The two deaths reported today followed the police response to a shooting at a city address. Police said one man attempting to flee the scene pointed a weapon at a police officer who commanded him to stop.

Police said they don't know if the man suffered a fatal wound from the officer's gun or from the shooting scene.

The other death involved a man who showed up at Danville Regional Medical Center and later died of his injuries.
