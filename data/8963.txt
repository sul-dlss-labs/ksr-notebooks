LAUREL, MS (WDAM) - Harvey Lee Fenderson, 38, is dead after being shot in the head by a Laurel Police Officer.

Authorities will not release the names of the officers who shot Fenderson, but they said two men are on administrative leave with pay until the investigation is complete.
