A suspect wanted for burglary was shot Saturday at Landry Properties just off Highway 30 and Penn St. in Gonzales.

The man was chased to the property by police where he locked himself inside of one of the apartments. Police say the man armed himself and refused to give up.

The SWAT team was called into the apartment where the suspect opened fire. The SWAT team returned fire and killed the suspect.

Gonzales Police Chief Bill Landry says they have run into the suspect before. The suspects name has not been released.
