Authorities have identified a man involved in a traffic accident with a Council Bluffs police cruiser on Interstate 29 Saturday afternoon as Bertram Ellis, 72, of Houston, Texas.

Ellis was pronounced dead at the hospital. Officer Josh Horner, who was injured in the crash, has been released from the hospital.

The accident happened at approximately 3:30 p.m.

Investigators say Officer Horner, 39, was involved in traffic control on I-29 southbound near 25th Street. He was parked on the side of the road when police say his vehicle was rear-ended by Ellis' vehicle.

Horner is a seven-year veteran of the department. Authorities have not specified the extent of his injuries.

Police continue to investigate the circumstances of the crash.
