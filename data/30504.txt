T.B.I investigating an officer involved shooting in Bradley County

BRADLEY CO., Tenn. (WDEF)- The T.B.I is investigating an officer involved shooting that happened Friday evening in Bradley County.

The incident happened before 6 p.m. last night.

Investigators say that Bradley County Sheriff’s deputies responded to a mental health call in the 200 block of Spring Branch Road.

Deputies say that when they arrived on scene, 54-year-old Michael Anthony Frederick had a weapon out, deputies fired their weapons and killed Frederick.

T.B.I will be investigating the shooting.

They will not be releasing the names of the deputies involved.
