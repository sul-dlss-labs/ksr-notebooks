Dan Scanlan

A driver killed March 23 in a fiery crash on Arlington Road while fleeing from police has been identified as a 28-year-old Jacksonville man, according to the Sheriff's Office.

Jermaine Lechardo Plummer, whose last known address was on Knottingham Trace Lane in the Sandalwood neighborhood, was speeding and running red lights before officers got involved in the 2:30 a.m. chase, according to Investigations Division Chief Adam Brown.

The chase began when a police officer at Bert and Lillian roads saw a car speeding north on Arlington before it ran two red lights, police said. The officer tried to catch the car but lost sight of it until it careened off the road, hit a retaining wall and became airborne before crashing and bursting into flames at Lillian Road.
