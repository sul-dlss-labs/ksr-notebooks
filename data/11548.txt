A new police report says that the Cedar Rapids man who died in a Marion motorcycle chase was traveling up to 124 mph while intoxicated.

The Gazette reports that 26-year-old Andrew S. Lown had a blood alcohol content of 0.186 — over twice the legal limit of .08 — when he allegedly sped away from a Linn County Sheriff's deputy.

The original incident occurred July 9 at about 11 p.m.
