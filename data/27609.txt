A man died Tuesday, Feb. 25, after a pursuit ended in a fiery crash in Upland.

Upland police officers attempted to stop the driver of a white BMW sedan, but it took off at a high rate of speed, Sgt. Moe Duran said. The driver lost control near San Antonio Avenue and 24th Street, where the car struck a power pole and caught fire.

The man was unconscious and first responders attempted life saving measures, Duran said, but he died as a result of his injuries.

The reason police attempted to pull over his vehicle was not immediately known.
