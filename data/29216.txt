An on-duty Boyce Police Department officer hit and killed a pedestrian early Tuesday morning on La. Highway 1, according to Louisiana State Police.

The unidentified male pedestrian was "standing with a bicycle near the white fog line in the southbound lane" when the officer's Dodge Charger hit him around 2 a.m.

The man died at the scene. The officer was not injured.

An investigation is continuing.
