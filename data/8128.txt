Chicago police were involved in a shooting Wednesday night that left one man dead on the West Side, authorities said.

The Cook County medical examiner's office said it was called to attend to one body, but could not provide additional information.

Mark Payne, spokesman for the Independent Police Review Authority, said police shot and killed a man and an officer was injured but not shot. He said the shooting happened about 7 p.m. in an alley about a half-block north of the 4300 block of West Washington Boulevard.
