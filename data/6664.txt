An unidentified man died in custody Saturday night after scuffling with officers on a CTA bus, Chicago police said. Officers were called to the 7900 block of South Vincennes Avenue after the man caused a disturbance aboard the bus, Officer John Mirabelli said. He was arrested and taken to the Gresham District police station, 7808 S. Halsted St., where he was found unresponsive upon arrival, the officer said.
