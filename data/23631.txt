CUYAHOGA FALLS, Ohio -- A Cuyahoga Falls man died of a self-inflicted gunshot wound Monday afternoon following a standoff with SWAT, police said.

Officers were called before 2:30 p.m. to a home in the 1200 block of Curtis Avenue for a man inside with possible weapons, Cuyahoga Falls police said in a news release.

The SWAT team was called and later found the 48-year-old man inside the home, authorities said.

The incident required police to close off a portion of the street.

Investigators did not identify the man.
