UPDATE: 10:50 p.m.

The crime scene has been turned over to the Shasta County District Attorney's Office, police said.

Police were in contact with the Coroner's Office.

Traffic into the Gold Hills Subdivision was being screened by police to only let residents through.

ORIGINAL STORY

Redding police are in the 1800 block of Gold Hills Drive this evening where a suicidal subject is sitting in a car.

Officers are closing off the street and telling neighbors to stay in their homes.

Police said about 8:30 p.m. that the man had a gun to his head. Later he told officers he was also on the phone to his wife.
