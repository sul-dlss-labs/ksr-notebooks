Funeral to be held for Roshad McIntosh, 19-year-old shot by Chicago police

CHICAGO (WLS) -- Services will be held Saturday for a Roshad McIntosh, a 19-year-old man shot and killed by Chicago police.

McIntosh's funeral starts at 10 a.m. at Pleasant Grove Baptist Church on the city's West Side.

The teen was shot and killed on Aug. 24 during a confrontation with police. Officers say they were questioning McIntosh when he ran, turned and then pointed a gun at them.

The Independent Police Review Authority is investigating.
