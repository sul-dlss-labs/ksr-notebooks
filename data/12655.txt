Man Shot & Killed By Police Identified

Baltimore City Police have released the name of the man who was killed as he exchanged gunfire with police in Canton on Thursday night.

Police say 42-year-old John Monroe was killed as he engaged police in a "running gun battle" outside a home in the 3400-block of Foster Avenue.

Police had responded to a domestic dispute at the home at around midnight Thursday, where officers found two women who had been shot.
