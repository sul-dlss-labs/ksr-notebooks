Services are planned for 2 p.m. today at the Trahan Funeral Chapel, 256 N. Madison, for the man killed in a Friday crash in Bay County.

Ryan G. Koebke, 30, died in a head-on collision after a high-speed police chase on Friday evening. Bay County Sheriff's deputies were pursuing Koebke after he was involved in a crash at State Park and Wheeler when, shortly after 6 p.m., Koebke's vehicle collided head-on with a car while driving west on Beaver near Fraser.

Koebke died at the scene. The driver of the other car, 49-year-old Rodney R. Hawkins of Kawkawlin, suffered minor injuries.

Koebke is survived by his wife, Cathy, stepson Elijah Budry and mother and stepfather, Linda and Daryl Utt.
