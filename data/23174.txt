UPDATE 8/7/17 @ 3:40 p.m.

SOUTH CHARLESTON, W.Va. (WSAZ) -- A body found Saturday in the Kanawha River has been identified as an inmate who escaped police custody a couple days earlier, the Kanawha County Sheriff's Office reports.

Deputies say Howard Stiltner, 43, of Prennar, West Virginia, died from apparent drowning.

Stiltner's body was found near Riverwalk Plaza in South Charleston. His hands were handcuffed behind his back when it was located.

Stiltner was arrested Thursday for obstructing an officer, but he ran from the patrol car while walking into the police station to be booked. Despite a search that included a K-9, officers were unable to find Stiltner. Deputies say his identity was unknown at the time, saying he had provided false information about his name and date of birth.

An autopsy was conducted after Stiltner's body was found.
