A Bronx man fell to his death on Tuesday night as he tried to escape through a window when police officers arrived at his door with an arrest warrant, the police said yesterday. The man, James Harris, 42, of 1900 Grand Concourse, was pronounced dead at the scene. The police said that Mr. Harris was being sought on charges of trademark counterfeiting and that he tried to escape when officers knocked on the door of his fourth-floor apartment.
