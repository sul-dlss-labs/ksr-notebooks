HILLSIDE — Authorities have identified the passenger killed in a stolen car crash on Route 22 as an Orange resident.

Regina Rowland, 45, was a passenger in a stolen Ford F-250 that crashed on Route 22 eastbound in Hillside on Friday, according to the Union County Prosecutor's Office.

The driver, David Graham, attempted to flee the scene but was apprehended and charged with aggravated manslaughter, eluding and unlawful taking of a motor vehicle, Acting Union County Prosecutor Grace Park said.

Graham, 42, of Newark, lost control of the truck, struck a pole near Bloy Street and crashed near the township's border with Newark, officials said.

Officials from the prosecutor's office did not say why Rowland was in the vehicle.

The Union County Prosecutor's Office Homicide Task Force is handling the investigation.
