GREEN BAY, Wis. (WFRV) – Police are investigating an incident on Green Bay’s west side.

According to authorities, an officer was stabbed by a male subject when they responded to a welfare check on a suicidal male.

Officers then shot and killed the subject. The officer’s injuries are not believed to be life threatening.

The investigation into this incident is just the beginning and is expected to take some time.

Police say they do not believe there is any ongoing danger at this time.

This investigation will be handled by the State Department of Criminal Investigation (DCI).
