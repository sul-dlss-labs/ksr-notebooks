An Easley man was shot and killed by police early Sunday morning in Greenville after they stopped his assault on another man.

According to WYFF, Greenville police broke up an altercation around 12:45 a.m. on Brown Street.

Police told the television station that Senovio "Novi" Maldonado, 19, of Easley was beating another man with a baseball bat. Maldonado allegedly then charged at police with the bat and the two officers on scene fired at him.
