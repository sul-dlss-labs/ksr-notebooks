1 man dead after officer-involved shooting in Signal Hill

SIGNAL HILL, Calif. (KABC) -- One man is dead after a shooting involving Signal Hill police officers Monday.

The officer-involved shooting happened just before 1 p.m. in the 1000 block of Willow Street in Signal Hill.

Few details were being released, but a man was dead at the scene. No officers were injured.

The Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department is aiding in the investigation.
