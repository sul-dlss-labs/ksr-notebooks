Woman killed by officers after setting fire to home, VSP says; brother responds

Chelsae L. Clevenger-Kirk was killed in an officer-involved shooting in the early morning hours of Sunday, Nov. 7, 2021. Virginia State Police say a Dept. of Wildlife Resources Conservation Police Officer attempted to make a traffic stop on Clevenger-Kirk, but she proceeded home without pulling over. A standoff ensued, and troopers say Clevenger-Kirk set her house on fire before exiting with a firearm and being shot by responding law enforcement, VSP says. (Credit: Kelsey Childress/WSET)
