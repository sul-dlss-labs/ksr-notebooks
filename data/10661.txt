The Associated Press

DELPHOS — The Kansas Highway Patrol says a man has been killed in a shootout with troopers near a north-central grain elevator.

KAKE-TV (http://bit.ly/pjHYA7) reports the incident began around 10:20 a.m. Wednesday at the Delphos Co-op in Ottawa County.

The patrol says the man was walking near the co-op but ran away when employees tried to approach him. Troopers and sheriff’s officers were called and spotted the man walking near a creek west of Delphos.

Authorities say officers tried to approach the man but he ran again. The Highway Patrol says troopers exchanged gunfire with the man after finding him in a creek bed.

The man’s name has not been released. The Kansas Bureau of Investigation has taken over the case.
