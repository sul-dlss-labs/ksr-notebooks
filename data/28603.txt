Ocala Star-Banner

A man was shot and killed by deputies Saturday night after he pointed a firearm at law enforcement as they responded to a domestic disturbance in Southeast Marion County, according to a post on the Marion County Sheriff’s Office Facebook page.

The name of the victim and officers involved has not yet been released.

According to MCSO Public Information Officer Zach Moore, the incident occurred after deputies responded to a call in the vicinity of Larch Road and Southeast 58th Avenue sometime after 7:30 p.m. Saturday.

When asked about the deputy’s status and whether the shooting was related to the domestic disturbance Moore said he could not comment due to an ongoing investigation by the Florida Department of Law Enforcement.
