An officer fatally shot a man who attacked police with a knife in Fontana on Friday night, March 13, authorities said.

About 10:05 p.m., a man made several calls to police, threatening to kill officers with a knife, according to a written statement from the Fontana Police Department.

Officers went to the 9700 block of Kempster Avenue and found the man. He became enraged and attacked officers, leading to an officer-involving shooting, the statement said.

No officers were injured.

The man, identified as Andrew Driver, was taken to Kaiser Permanente hospital, where he died, officials said.
