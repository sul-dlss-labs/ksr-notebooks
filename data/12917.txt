TUCSON, AZ (Tucson News Now) - Police have identified the woman who was killed in an officer-involved shooting on Saturday.

Tucson Police say they opened fire at 24-year-old Renee Witham and a man at a midtown home after she aimed a gun at the officers.

According to police, three officers fired their weapons at Witham after she refused to drop the gun after several requests to do so.

The man with Witham was also shot. He was taken to a local hospital with non-life threatening injuries.
