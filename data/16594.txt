JORDAN SIGLER

The Texas Rangers are investigating a death that happened Tuesday after a man fled a traffic stop by Levelland police, according to a statement by the Texas Department of Public Safety.

During the stop around 5 p.m., Miguel Mendoza, 29, fled on foot and ingested methamphetamine and synthetic marijuana, according to the statement.

Mendoza was taken by EMS to Covenant hospital in Levelland, where he died, the statement said.

No other information was released.

Mendoza had a previous misdemeanor conviction in 2014 for theft between $50 and $500, according to Lubbock County Court records.
