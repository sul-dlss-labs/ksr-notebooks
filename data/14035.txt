SYLACAUGA, Alabama -- A police chase that started in Alexander City Tuesday ended with an apparent suicide in Sylacauga, according to The Daily Home.

Courtney McKinney, 23, was pulled over on U.S. Highway 280 near the Alexander City Wal-Mart for driving too closely around 5 p.m. Tuesday, police told The Daily Home.

He had a license but no insurance, the tag was found not to belong to the Ford Mustang, and officers smelled marijuana and saw a weapon in the car, police said.

McKinney took off and officers chased him into Sylacauga, where he was cornered, tried to flee on foot, and turned his gun on himself, according to The Daily Home.

The Alabama Bureau of Investigation is looking into the incident.
