A parolee fatally shot by two state parole agents Wednesday night has been identified as Anthony Castellano.

The county Medical Examiner’s Office released Castellano’s name Saturday and said he died of multiple gunshot wounds.

The parole agents and San Diego police were looking for Castellano at the Best Western Plus San Diego/Miramar Hotel on Kearny Mesa Road. He allegedly pointed a handgun at the uniformed agents, who opened fire on him. He was taken to a hospital, where he died about 8 p.m.
