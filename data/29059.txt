KILLEEN, Texas (KWTX) - Killeen police are investigating after they say a man shot at an officer before taking his own life.

Callers reported the man was waving a gun around Tuesday evening in a neighborhood on Caprice Drive.

According to Killeen Police Chief Charles Kimble, the man fired a shot at an officer and then took off running.

At some point the man turned the gun on himself.

No officers were injured.

No further information was available.
