DOTHAN -- Dothan police say a Georgia man who was a suspect in an armed robbery shot and killed himself.

Police Chief J.C. West said Thursday that law enforcement personnel, including U.S. marshals, tried to apprehend the man after he was located, but he shot himself in the head

Barbour County Coroner Chip Chapman said 28-year-old Lextrell Williams of Lumpkin, Ga., was pronounced dead at a hospital.

Police say he was being sought in connection with the robbery of a gas station early Monday morning.
