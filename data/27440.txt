Pedestrian hit by Spartanburg police officer in crash dies days later, identified, coroner says

A pedestrian has died days later after being hit by a Spartanburg police officer on a busy road, coroner says.(Video above: story previously reported)Spartanburg Coroner Rusty Clevenger said the pedestrian, Sherry Lee Garrison, 54, died.Clevenger said Garrison died at Spartanburg Regional Medical Center at about 11 p.m. Saturday.The officer was involved in the crash with Garrison Thursday night, according to Maj. Art Littlejohn, with the Spartanburg Police Department.The accident happened around 7:15 p.m. on West Main Street near Forest Street, Littlejohn said.
