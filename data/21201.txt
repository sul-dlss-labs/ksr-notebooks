RELATED: Ann Arbor motorcyclist killed in morning crash

Police have identified the motorcyclist killed early Friday as 24-year-old Brandon Preslar of Ann Arbor.

Pittsfield Township Police Lt. Sean McCormick said Preslar ran a red light on his motorcycle and crashed into another car at about 1:30 a.m.Preslar was southbound on Golfside Road when he went through the red light at Washtenaw Avenue, striking the other vehicle broadside, authorities said.

The driver of the other vehicle was transported to St. Joseph Mercy Hospital with minor injuries.

Police are still trying to determine if any other factors contributed to the crash.
