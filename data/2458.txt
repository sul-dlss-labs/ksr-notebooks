(New Orleans-AP) -- A 25-year-old pregnant woman and her unborn child were killed in a traffic accident last night with an off-duty New Orleans police officer. The incident happened just before 10:30 p.m.

Authorities say Sabrina Desilva was broadsided by Officer Frederick Carter, who was driving his personal vehicle. Police say Desilva failed to yield at a stop sign, but her family members said she had stopped.

Desilva and her unborn child died at the hospital. Carter was injured in the accident, but his condition is unknown. No charges have been filed, but an investigation is continuing.
