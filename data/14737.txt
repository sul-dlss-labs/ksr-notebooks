A 40-year-old Colton resident was killed late Wednesday, July 2, when he crashed his motorcycle after police tried to pull him over, the San Bernardino County coroner said.

About 11:40 p.m., a news release said, a Colton police officer tried to make a traffic stop on Christopher Negrete on North Mt. Vernon Avenue near East Laurel Street. Negrete continued south on North Mt. Vernon a short distance until his motorcycle struck a curb in the center divider near East Lemon Street.
