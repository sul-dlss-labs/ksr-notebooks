COLUMBUS – The Franklin County Sheriff’s Office has identified the person who was shot and killed in a deputy-involved shooting in west Columbus.

It happened just before 11:45 a.m. Monday in the Hilltop.

Detectives with the Franklin County Drug Task Force were conducting an investigation into a suspected heroin trafficking in the 400 block of S. Wayne Ave.

During the investigation, a detective with the task force was involved in a shooting incident with 58-year-old Hustes Davila.

The sheriff’s office said Davila was shot in the upper body by the detective and was pronounced dead at the scene.

The detective was not injured.
