(Santee) June 7, 2006 - A former Santee police officer has been sentenced to three years probation.

Brian Keith Brown pleaded guilty to reckless homicide in a fatal 2002 car crash. It happened on state highway 210.

John Donald Stroman, 61, of Bowman was killed.

Brown's police powers were suspended last year. State officials say they plan to permanently revoke his certification.
