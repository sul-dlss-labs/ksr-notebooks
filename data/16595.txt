JACKSON, Tenn. — The Tennessee Bureau of Investigation has arrested an officer with the Paris, Tennessee Police Department on first-degree murder charges.

On September 2, the TBI was called in to investigate the fatal injuries sustained by a 2-month-old boy named Gunner Page.

The nature of the injuries have not been released, but the child died less than a week later.

During the investigation, it was revealed the child’s father, Christopher Warren Page, was the individual responsible for his death.

Page was arrested on Thursday and booked into the Henry County Jail, and subsequently taken to the Carroll County Jail.

He has been charged with first-degree murder and aggravated child abuse.
