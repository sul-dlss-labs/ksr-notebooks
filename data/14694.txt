On Monday, June 23, at approximately 8:26 p.m., a Rhinelander Police Officer attempted to perform a traffic stop on a vehicle on Davenport Street for speeding. A pursuit ensued and continued to the intersection of Highway K and Forest Loop Road where the vehicle left the roadway and came to rest in the ditch area.

The 27-year-old male driver passed away at the scene of the crash. The subject was identified as Sean E. O’Corrain of Rhinelander.

The Oneida County Sheriff’s Office, Wisconsin State Patrol, and Oneida County Medical Examiner’s Office are continuing their investigation into the events surrounding the vehicle crash.
