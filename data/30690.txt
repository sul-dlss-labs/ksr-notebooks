Celina police and Texas Rangers are investigating a fatal officer-involved shooting in Celina Friday night.

Police were called out at 9 p.m., for a suspicious person call in the 100 block of S. Alabama Street.

Responding officers reported encountering an armed suspect in the apartment complex's parking lot. Officers then fired at the suspect.

No further details have been released about what the suspect and officers did before the shooting.

The suspect was taken to a Plano hospital, where he was pronounced dead.

No officers were injured, and those involved have been placed on administrative leave pending the outcome of the investigation.

Texas Rangers are assisting Celina PD with the investigation.
