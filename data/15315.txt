HARTSELLE, Alabama -- Alabama State Troopers are investigating a fatal accident involving an on-duty Hartselle police officer.

Hartselle police said Lt. James Holladay was on a routine patrol on Sparkman Street when a man crossed the street in front of him. Police said the car ran into the man just before 5 p.m. Wednesday. The pedestrian was taken to Decatur Morgan Hospital then transferred to Huntsville Hospital where he succumbed to his injuries.

Alabama State Troopers have taken over the investigation. They identified the victim as 71-year-old William Anderson of Cullman.
