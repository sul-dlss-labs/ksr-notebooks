GREENVILLE, AL (WSFA) - One man is dead after crashing his car during a police chase Friday.

Police tell WSFA 12 News that the wreck occurred on 185 North at mile marker 7 in front of the White Oak golf course in Greenville.

Officials say the suspect lost control of the car, forcing the car onto its side and into another car.

The suspect was ejected from the vehicle and pronounced dead.

Two passengers in the other car were taken to the hospital, and treated for minor injuries.

The case remains under investigation.
