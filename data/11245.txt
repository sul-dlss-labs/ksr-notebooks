BIRMINGHAM, Alabama -- An 18-year-old woman, who was a passenger in a car that sped away and crashed following an attempted police stop earlier this month, died this morning, according to the Jefferson County Coroner's Office.

Heather Parker, 18, died at UAB Hospital at 5 a.m. today, said Deputy Jefferson County Coroner Phillip Russell.

Parker had been injured in an April 16 crash on Forest Road between Hueytown and Pleasant Grove.

Parker was a passenger in a vehicle that had sped away after Pleasant Grove police had stopped it. The driver, Robert Fletcher, 28, of Leeds, lost control, the vehicle crashed into a tree and caught fire. Fletcher died April 22.
