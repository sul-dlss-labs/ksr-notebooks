A shooting early Thursday evening involving a law enforcement officer and a man has left one dead. Peter Daniel Grima, 65, of Andalusia has been identified as the victim. The shooting occurred at a residence on North Carter Road in Luverne.

Special Agents with the Alabama Law Enforcement Agency’s (ALEA) State Bureau of Investigation (SBI) are investigating a shooting at the request of Crenshaw County Sheriff Mickey Powell.

Once the investigation is complete, the findings will be turned over to the Crenshaw County District Attorney’s office.

This story will be updated as more information becomes available.
