MADISONVILLE (WATE) – Tennessee Bureau of Investigation is looking into a fatal shooting that occurred at an off-duty Monroe County reserve deputy’s home.

Investigators say the deputy heard something outside his home early Friday morning. He discovered David Scott Winkler, 44, trying to steal property. The deputy fired shots at the man’s vehicle after Winkler tried to hit him, according to the report.

Winkler was transported to Sweetwater Hospital and later died.

There is no more information at this time.
