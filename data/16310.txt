On July 4, 2015 Mr. Carrizales was involved in a domestic disturbance with his parents. Mr. Carrizales

reportedly had been using Cocaine and began hallucinating and becoming violent. Mr. Carrizales's young son intervened and tackled him to help gain control. Garland Police Officers arrived and were able to get Mr. Carrizales off of his son and then place him into custody. Mr. Carrizales collapsed moments later.

Medical attention was immediately sought and Mr. Carrizales was taken to Baylor at Garland Medical Center where he remained until his death. The Dallas County Medical Examiner performed an autopsy and results are pending.
