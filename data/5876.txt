Incident at 12818 Huntington Venture Drive

February 3, 2007 -- A suspect wanted for a murder in Huntsville, Texas, was found dead at 12818 Huntington Venture Drive about 4:35 a.m. today (Feb. 3).

The deceased suspect is identified as John Dennis Kizzee (b/m DOB: 1-15-59). He was pronounced dead at the scene from an apparent self-inflicted gunshot wound to the head. Houston Police Department Homicide Division Officers T. R. Cunningham and E. Carstens reported:

At the request of the Huntsville Police Department and the Texas Rangers, HPD was asked to arrest a suspect wanted in a February 1 murder in Huntsville. The arrest warrant was classified as being very high risk due to statements the suspect had made about being arrested.
