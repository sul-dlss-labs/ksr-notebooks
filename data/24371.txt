An officer-involved shooting left a man dead on June 28 in Russellville.

Russellville police responded to a call when Joshua Cartwright, 36, allegedly shot at bail bondsmen trying to bring him in on felony warrants. The suspect fired from inside the apartment when officers arrived at the 600 block of West K Street.

Surrounding apartment complexes were evacuated and police began to negotiate with the suspect. Assistance from the Arkansas State Police Tactical Unit was requested. He emerged from the apartment at 2:10 a.m. and began firing at police, according to a press release.
