UPDATE: Louisiana State Police have identified the man killed by St. Martin Parish deputies as 35-year-old Avery D. Metrejean of Morgan City.

The investigation is still ongoing.

***************************************

One man is dead after a police-involved shooting in Cade Monday night.

State Police Spokesperson Master Trooper Brooks David said St. Martin Parish Sheriff's Deputies were attempting to serve an arrest warrant for an individual at a home on Duchamp Road in Cade.

When deputies encountered a male wielding a gun, things turned violent. Deputies fired at the subject who was wounded by the officer's bullets.

The unidentified man was transported to a local hospital where he later died from his injuries.

David said Louisiana State Police detectives are working diligently to gather all the facts surrounding the situation.
