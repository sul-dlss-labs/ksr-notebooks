Harrisburg man killed in motorcycle crash

By: Christy Hendricks

WILLIAMSON COUNTY, Ill. - A Harrisburg man is dead after crashing a motorocycle in eastern Williamson County Tuesday night .

Authorities say 22-year-old Nathan Morgan was fleeing from the Saline County Sheriff's Department at the time of the crash.

A witness who was riding another motorcycle with Morgan said they were traveling about 130-140 mph just before the crash.
