WATERLOO, Iowa (AP) -- A Waterloo man was killed late Friday when his car collided with a marked Waterloo Police Department squad car at a city intersection.

Bernard Cornelius Simmons, 75, of Waterloo, died from injuries in the accident. Simmons was taken to Covenant Hospital where he later died from injuries suffered in the accident.

Officer Jeff Tyler, the driver of the squad car, was taken to Allen Hospital. A nursing supervisor said Tyler was treated and released.
