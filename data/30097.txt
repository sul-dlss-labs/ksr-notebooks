Sheriff: 3 dead after suspect steers sports car into oncoming traffic on Oregon Coast

A suspect reported speeding and driving eratically on the Oregon Coast crossed the double yellow line and intentionally crashed into an oncoming car after the suspect's car was involved in a minor collision with an Oregon State Police vehicle Thursday, the Coos County Sheriff's Office said. The crash killed the suspect and both people inside the oncoming car, authorities revealed Friday. (Daniel P.)
