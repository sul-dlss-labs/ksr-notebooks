A Lancaster man died early Saturday morning fleeing Lebanon police.

According to a release from Lebanon Police Chief Tod Breiner, police became involved in a "less than two mile vehicle pursuit that started in (Lebanon) city" at 2:41 a.m. Dec. 30.

The pursuit ended when Brandon Small, 25, of Lancaster struck an electrical pole in North Lebanon Township. Small died from the injuries he sustained in the accident, according to Breiner.

The North Lebanon Township Police Department is handling the investigation of the motor vehicle accident.
