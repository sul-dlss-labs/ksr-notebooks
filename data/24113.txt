LOUISVILLE, Ky. (WHAS11) -- A man has died after being shot by police Wednesday night.

The Jefferson County Coroner's Office said 42-year-old Isaac Jackson died of gunshot wounds.

According to police, officers received a call about a disturbance in the 400 block of N 42nd Street. When officers arrived at the home, they entered to find the suspect, Jackson, armed with multiple knives.

Jackson reportedly attacked the officers, injuring one of them with a knife. Another officer fired and struck Jackson, killing him.

The injured officer was taken to the hospital. The extent of his injuries is unknown and he has not been identified.
