An Alabama sate trooper was involved in an officer-involved shooting in Sylacauga Wednesday night, according to the Alabama Law Enforcement Agency.

The subject involved is dead and the officer was taken to a hospital for further evaluation, said Cpl. Jimmy Harrell Jr.

ALEA officials did not provide details of the shooting, but said a press conference will be held Thursday morning.

The press conference will be at the Birmingham ALEA-SBI Office at 9 a.m. Harrell and Lt. John Riley will speak at the press conference.

Additional details about the shooting were not immediately available.
