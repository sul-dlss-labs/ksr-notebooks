A man was shot and killed Friday at a grocery store in McDowell County and the alleged shooter in the case, who initially barricaded himself inside of a residence, later took his own life, according to West Virginia State Police officials. The incident occurred early Friday afternoon at a Sunamco grocery store near Jolo, Sgt. R.A. Maddy, district commander of the McDowell, Mercer and Monroe county detachments, said.
