Kami Stevens, a 40-year-old white woman, was shot and killed by Long Beach police at 1021 Coronado Ave. on Wednesday, Dec. 26, according to authorities.

Officers had gone to the location after getting a call about a dispute. When they arrived, they found Stevens distraught and on the roof, according to a police news release.

Officers could see that she had a handgun and tried to negotiate with her, the release said. Officers said she pointed her weapon at them and they fired at her.

She was taken to a hospital where she died.
