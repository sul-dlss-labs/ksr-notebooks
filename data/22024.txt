ELIZABETH — One of two robbery suspects was shot and killed by police outside a hotel this morning, authorities said.

The shooting occurred just after 4 a.m. at the Knight’s Inn on Spring Street near Newark Airport when police responded to a robbery report. The officers were confronted by two armed suspects and opened fire. Both suspects, Jahind Davis, 25, of Newark and Jaquil Spruill, 18, of Plainfield were struck.

Davis was taken to Trinitas Hospital in traumatic arrest and was pronounced dead upon arrival. Spruill was taken to University Hospital in Newark where he was listed in critical condition. There were no injuries to responding officers, and the incident is being investigated by the Union County Prosecutor’s Office.
