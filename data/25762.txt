Authorities responded to a home on Dockhouse Lane, off Y Highway in Stone County for a report of an open 911 line. When deputies arrived, they found a female standing in the road. While talking to the woman a male walked outside the residence and fired multiple shots at them, hitting one police vehicle three times. No deputies were injured.

The male went back inside, and deputies secured a perimeter around the residence. Contact with the suspect was attempted, but efforts were unsuccessful.

During the standoff, deputies heard gunshots from inside the home. They made entry into the home and found the male suspect dead from a self-inflicted gunshot wound.

The deceased suspect was identified as a 27-year-old from Springfield.
