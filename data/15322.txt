Updated at 1:28 p.m. Monday by Tasha Tsiaperas:

A 57-year-old man who was fatally shot Friday night inside a Sansom Park home was killed by a police officer, police officials said Monday.

Officers responded to a domestic disturbance call around 10 p.m. Friday at a house in the 5800 block of Jacksboro Highway. David Wyatt Grayson was armed with a knife and holding a woman inside the home, Sansom Park police officials said.

Grayson refused to let the woman go or to leave the house, so officers went inside the house, officials said.

One officer fired shots at Grayson, who was later pronounced dead at the scene, according to police records.

Grayson died from gunshot wounds to the neck and chest, according to the Tarrant County medical examiner.

The Texas Rangers and the Tarrant County Sheriff's Office are investigating the shooting. The name of the officer who shot Grayson has not been released.
