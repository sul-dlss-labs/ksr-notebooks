A Phenix City man is charged with murder after an April police chase resulted in the death of a teenager.

Dennis Hoskins was indicted for the death of Katherine Goins.

Police say the 18-year old Goins and another woman were in Hoskins car when officers approached it April 17th at a gas station. Authorities say that's when Hoskins sped off. A brief police chase ended when the car crashed into a utility pole.
