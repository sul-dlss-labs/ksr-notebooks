Two Appleton police officers shot and killed a man overnight as he charged at them with a pair of swords, the Appleton Post Crescent is reporting.

Police were called to the home to deal with a domestic situation. Armando Aponte, 35, died in the shooting as he lunged at the officers with the weapons, police said.

It was the first fatal shooting involving an Appleton police officer in 24 years, the paper reported.
