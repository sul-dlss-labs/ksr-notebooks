A Shreveport man involved in a car wreck late Tuesday night has died of his injuries.

Latravious L. Stewart of the 1500 block of Doris Street died Wednesday at University Health Shreveport, where he was taken after being ejected from an SUV that crashed, police said.

The wreck happened on Creswell Avenue near Kings Highway when the SUV fled from Shreveport police during a traffic stop. The SUV later crashed into a utility pole.

Police are still looking for a second person they say was inside the SUV at the time of the crash. They believe he may have fled on foot after the wreck.

Call Caddo Crime Stoppers at 318-673-7373 or visit www.lockemup.org if you have information about the incident.
