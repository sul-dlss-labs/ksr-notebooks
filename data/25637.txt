Video of a deadly police shooting following a 13-hour stand-off in Struthers shows the suspect’s gun fall from an upper-story window.

In the video, which may be disturbing for some viewers, you can hear the gunfire and see 48-year-old Brandon Schmitt’s rifle drop out of the home on Fifth Street.
