The San Juan County Sheriff’s Office released video footage of deputies shooting an Albuquerque man. On Aug. 31, officers were responding to multiple cars being stolen near Farmington.Police eventually found Marco Benito and chased him, after he jumped out of a stolen SUV.Benito went into an irrigation ditch, where officers shot and killed the 36-year-old.
