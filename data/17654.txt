A pedestrian struck by a Birmingham police patrol vehicle has died.

The Jefferson County Coroner's Office today identified the man as David Dementarius Canty. He was 51.

The crash happened about 9 p.m. Thursday in the 6800 block of First Avenue North. Police said Canty ran out into traffic and in front of the patrol vehicle. The officer, they said, didn't have time to stop.

Canty was taken to UAB Hospital where he was pronounced dead at 10:41 p.m.

The crash remains under investigation.
