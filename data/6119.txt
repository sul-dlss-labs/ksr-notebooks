MESA - A Maricopa County Deputy Sheriff fatally wounded a man at a campground near Bartlett Lake.

Deputies responded about 4 a.m. Sunday to a report of a man firing a pistol at the Needle Rock campground, between Rio Verde and Bartlett Lake, said Sheriff's office spokesman Deputy Doug Matteson. When deputies arrived, there was a confrontation and one deputy shot the man, Matteson said.

The suspect has been identified as Eduardo Alvardo. He died about 1 p.m. Sunday. His age and hometown wasn't immediately available.
