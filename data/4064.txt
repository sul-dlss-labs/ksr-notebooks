Area police were involved in two shootings yesterday, one of them resulting in the death of a 37-year-old man.

A Baltimore County police officer fatally shot Steven J. McGilton in the chest after he approached the officer brandishing a metal pipe, police said.

The incident occurred about 4:45 p.m. at McGilton's home in the 11200 block of Bird River Grove Road in Essex. Police said McGilton, who had a criminal record, failed to respond to repeated requests to drop the pipe.

In an unrelated incident, a Baltimore City police officer responding to a domestic disturbance call shot a man wielding a knife. The man was shot in the arm about 4:40 p.m. in the 2100 block of Herbert St. in Walbrook.

Police said the man ignored commands to drop his weapon and lunged at the officer. The man's injuries were not life-threatening, police said.
