EAST ALTON, IL (KTVI) – A motorcyclist was killed overnight in East Alton Illinois. East Alton police say 44-year-old Bruce Norfleet of Alton was riding his motorcycle westbound on East St. Louis Avenue when he ran off the road and struck a building.

Norfleet suffered extensive injuries and was transported to Alton Memorial Hospital where he later died from his injuries.

Both the East Alton Police Department and the Illinois State Police are investigating the accident.
