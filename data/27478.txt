A 70-year-old man shot another man and then himself late Sunday as he barricaded himself in his apartment with a young girl, police said.

The shooting and hostage situation prompted a SWAT standoff about 11 p.m. Sunday at an apartment building on West 28th Street and Division Avenue in the Ohio City neighborhood, according to police.

The 70-year-old man initially holed up inside the apartment with the 12-year-old girl, police said. Officers found the 70-year-old man dead inside the home.

A man 30-year-old man who suffered a gunshot wound to the chest in the apartment was taken to MetroHealth where he was pronounced dead, according to police. The 12-year-old girl was not hurt, police said.

Police said the 70-year-old man was a family friend who was living with the 30-year-old man, a 32-year-old woman and the girl.

A 32-year-old woman told police that the 30-year-old man had been shot inside the apartment and that her daughter and the 70-year-old man were still inside.

Officers went into the apartment and carried out the 30-year-old man outside. They also found the 12-year-old girl in her bedroom and escorted her to safety, according to police.

The officers searched for the 70-year-old man and heard a gunshot. A SWAT team surrounded the apartment and eventually went inside, finding the man dead of an apparent gunshot wound to the head, according to police.

The identities of the two men have not been released.

Cleveland police are investigating the circumstances surrounding the incident.
