LEOMINSTER — A 24-year-old man was seriously injured when his ATV collided with a landscaping truck pulling a trailer about 4:30 Monday afternoon in the area of 220 Litchfield St.

Police have not released the identity of the ATV driver. He was taken to UMass Memorial Medical Center — University Campus in Worcester.

The accident is under investigation by state Environmental Police and Leominster police.
