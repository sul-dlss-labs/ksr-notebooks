The pedestrian who was struck by a marked police vehicle in north Phoenix Tuesday was identified as 18-year-old Jordan Black.

Black was walking in the roadway early Tuesday morning, according to a Phoenix police spokesman.

About 4 a.m., Officer Justin Futrell was driving near Seventh Street and Deer Valley Road when the accident occurred, said Sgt. Vince Lewis, a Phoenix police spokesman.

Black was transported to a hospital, where he died, Lewis said.
