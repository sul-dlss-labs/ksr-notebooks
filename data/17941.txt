LOUISVILLE, KY (WAVE) - The SWAT situation in the Pleasure Ridge Park neighborhood has ended in the death of a man by a self-inflicted gunshot wound.

A SWAT team was called to a domestic situation on Santa Fe Trail in PRP.

The call came in just after 4 a.m. Sunday morning. According to MetroSafe, a brother and sister got into an argument, and that's when 911 was called.
