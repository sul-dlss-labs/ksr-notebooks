MEMPHIS, TN (WMC) - The Shelby County Sheriff’s Office says a man committed suicide Thursday as deputies served a warrant.

Deputies were at the home near Fizer Avenue and Getwell Road to execute the warrant when they heard a single gunshot.

According to the sheriff’s office, the deputies entered the home and found the man with a self-inflicted gunshot wound. He was pronounced dead at the scene.

The sheriff’s office hasn’t released the man’s name or the reason for the warrant.
