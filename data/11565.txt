PARMA HEIGHTS -- A Brook Park man was killed when the vehicle he was driving struck a tree near 15475 Holland Road in Brook Park early in the morning of July 15.

Neil Guercia, 22, was pronounced dead at the scene following a high-speed chase that began on Mandalay Drive.

According to a press release from the Parma Heights police, on July 15 at approximately 4:28 a.m. officers saw a vehicle leaving the area of Mandalay Drive traveling at a high rate of speed and failing to stop for several traffic control devices.

The vehicle continued at a high rate of speed turning west on Huffman Road, past W. 130th Street and into Brook Park, where after crossing a set of railroad tracks and going airborne, struck a tree located near 14525 Holland Road.

The incident remains under investigation.
