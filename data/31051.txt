A fatal officer-involved shooting has been reported after an incident in Stevenson Ranch.

The shooting occurred at about 4:20 p.m. Sunday during a disturbance call on the 24000 block of Chicory Court in Stevenson Ranch, according to initial reports.

The shooting did involve a law enforcement officer, according to Deputy Natalie Arriaga with the Santa Clarita Valley Sheriff’s Station.

Medical personal were called to the scene by Sheriff’s officials to evaluate the incident, however, no additional details were available as of 4:32 p.m. Sunday, according to Los Angeles County Fire Department spokesperson Miguel Ornelas.

Initial reports stated that the suspect had been seen hanging out of a window with a possible weapon pointed at law enforcement.

The suspect, a male adult, was pronounced dead at the scene of the incident, according to Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department Lt. Charles Calderaro.
