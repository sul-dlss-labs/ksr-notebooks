POLICE Department

Investigation into Incident at 13200 Rockglen

October 23, 2015

- Houston police are awaiting autopsy results in the death of a man who was found in medical distress at 13200 Rockglen about 8:50 p.m. on Thursday (Oct.22).

The unidentified white male was transported to East Houston Regional Medical Center. His identity and cause of death are pending verification by the Harris County Institute of Forensic Sciences.

HPD Homicide Division Officers M. Arroyo and J. Nielsen reported:

HPD patrol officers responded to a call at the above address and found HFD personnel crews on the scene and a man acting erratically. While in the presence of both HFD and HPD, the man became medically unresponsive. He was transported to the hospital where he was pronounced dead.

As is customary when an individual dies in the presence of HPD officers, this case will be investigated by the HPD Homicide and Internal Affairs Divisions.
