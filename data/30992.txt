Chicago police fatally shot a person in the city's Englewood neighborhood late Sunday morning, according to officials.

At approximately 10:55 a.m. in the 6500 block of South Harvard, police said officers responded to a domestic disturbance, encountering a 28-year old man with a knife.

Police shot the man, who was later pronounced dead, according to officials.

The officer involved will be placed on administrative duties for 30 days, police said.
