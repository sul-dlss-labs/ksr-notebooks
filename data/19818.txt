A man died in an officer-involved shooting near Kyle on Friday, according to Oglala Sioux tribal officials.

A pursuit started Friday night by Bennett County law enforcement crossed over onto the reservation where tribal police took over the chase. The male suspect led police to Allen and then toward Kyle.

The suspect fired multiple shots at officers during the chase, according to a statement from OST President Scott Weston.

The suspect was shot and killed near Kyle by tribal Department of Public Safety officers. His name was not released.

The incident is currently under investigation by the FBI, Bureau of Indian Affairs and the OST DPS.
