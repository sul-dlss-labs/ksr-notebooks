Kentucky State Police are investigating the shooting death of a knife-wielding man by two officers in Owensboro. State police said 29-year-old Lucas Anderson died from gunshot wounds in the shooting Friday night. Police said Owensboro Police Department officers responded to a domestic argument call. Police said the officers saw Anderson grab a woman's hair and put a knife to her throat. Police said officers negotiated with Anderson to get him to drop the knife and release the woman. Police said Anderson threw the woman aside and charged two officers with the knife. The officers, identified as Mike Matthews and Gary Mattingly, fired their pistols multiple times, striking Anderson. The woman was hospitalized with cuts on her head and neck. The officers have been placed on paid administrative leave.
