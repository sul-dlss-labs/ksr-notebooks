A St. Louis man being pursued by police died after his car crashed on the Interstate 70 eastbound ramp to Jennings Station Road about 9:45 p.m. Friday.

Jay L. Latchison, 47, was pronounced dead at the scene, according to the Missouri Highway Patrol.

St. Louis County Police said Latchison was driving a vehicle believed to have been involved in a purse snatching in Bridgeton.

Latchison was traveling eastbound on Interstate 70 in a 2006 Cadillac CTS when he traveled off the right side of the roadway and struck a traffic signal box and overturned.

Debris from his vehicle struck a southbound 2004 Buick Century driven by Daphne D. Green, 40, of St. Louis. She was not injured.
