February 7, 2004

Reported by: Rhonda Kitchens

The Calcasieu Parish Sheriff's Office is investigating the early morning death of a man being booked into the correctional facility.

According to a Sheriff's Department spokesman, 22-year-old Keith Richardson was arrested by the Westlake Police Department early this morning for posession of CDS II.

They say Richardson first became ill while at the Westlake Police Department, but refused medical treatment. He was then transported to the Calcasieu Correctional Facility where he once again became ill.

While being checked by a nurse, Richardson became unresponsive and CPR was performed.

He died later in a local hospital..
