EVANSVILLE, IN (WFIE) - One man has died after we're told he drove his motorcycle into oncoming traffic.

It happened at the intersection of Garvin Street and Washington Avenue.

Before the crash, we're told officers tried to stop Jason Redman for traffic violations.

Police began a pursuit but said they eventually stopped the chase because of how fast Redman was going.

Minutes later, we're told Redman got into an accident where he hit a driver head-on and died.

There is no word yet on the condition of the other driver.

Police said the crash is still under investigation at this time.
