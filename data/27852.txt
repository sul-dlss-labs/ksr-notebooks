Grand jury: Oregon suspect fired 36 rounds into police cars before being shot, killed

Law enforcement officers were justified in shooting and killing a man who opened fire on state troopers and sheriff's deputies with a semiautomatic rifle, hitting 3 occupied police vehicles with three dozen rounds, the Klamath County Grand Jury reports. The shootout happened as law enforcement caught up to Matthew Brennon Goff on April 10. (OSP)
