MILFORD, MI - One person was killed and two others were injured when a police chase ended in a crash early Sunday, May 21, in the community of Milford in western Oakland County, according to a report by Click On Detroit.

Police attempted to stop a vehicle they believed had been stolen about 4 a.m. Sunday, but the vehicle fled and the driver eventually lost control and rolled over on westbound I-96 near Kensington Road, according to the report.

One person was killed in the crash and the matter remains under investigation, Click On Detroit reports.
