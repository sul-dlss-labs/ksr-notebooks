CENTENNIAL, Colo. (CBS4)- The man shot and killed in a deadly shooting near the Arapahoe County Sheriff's Office in Centennial was a former deputy.

The shooting happened about 9:45 p.m. Sunday at a street entrance to the parking lot.

(credit: CBS)

Before the shooting, a deputy exiting the parking lot observed a vehicle backed into the exit gate area. The deputy contacted the driver and observed that he had a gun. Two other deputies arrived almost immediately.

When the man turned the gun toward one of them, deputies discharged their weapons. No deputies were hurt.

(credit: CBS)

The shooting victim has been identified as Mark Bidon, 50. Bidon was a deputy at the Arapahoe County Sheriff's Office from 2000 to 2010, when he resigned.
