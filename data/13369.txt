FOX 8 received this news advisory from the New Orleans Police Dept. Friday night:

Around 8:30pm in the 600 block of City Park Avenue, for reasons unknown a 27-year-old male walked into the travel lane and was struck by one of our third district Police cruisers. The man was transported to the hospital where he was pronounced dead. The Officer was not injured. This case is currently being investigated by the Public Integrity Bureau and the Fatality Division. No additional information is available.

Monitor FOX8Live.com Saturday for updates on this story.
